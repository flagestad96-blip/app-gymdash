Gymdash

Privacy Policy

Gymdash is a 100% offline app. Your data never leaves your device.

Overview

Gymdash is a workout logging and program building app designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Effective date: February 10, 2026

Data Collection

Gymdash does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No crash reporting sent to external servers
  • No location data collected
  • No network requests made by the app

Data Storage

All data you enter into Gymdash is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. This includes:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights, RPE)
  • Training programs and exercise configurations
  • Body metrics (weight, progress photos)
  • Personal records and achievements
  • App settings and preferences

This data is only accessible to you and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Data Export & Backup

Gymdash provides built-in backup and export features (JSON and CSV). When you use these features, the exported files are saved to your device or shared through your device's native share sheet. No data is sent to Gymdash or any external service — you control where your exports go.

Progress Photos

If you choose to attach progress photos to body measurements, these images are stored locally on your device. Gymdash does not upload, process, or share your photos in any way.

Notifications

Gymdash may send local notifications for rest timers and workout reminders. These are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external push notification services.

Third-Party Services

Gymdash does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app makes zero network requests during normal use.

Children's Privacy

Gymdash does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no personal information is collected or transmitted, the app complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since Gymdash does not collect any data, changes are unlikely.
